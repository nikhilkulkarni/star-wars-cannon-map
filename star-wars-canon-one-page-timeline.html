Official era framing

A clean illustrated timeline of the Star Wars screen canon

This page aligns the official eras with character lifelines and the major films and series in one scrollable view. The chronology follows Lucasfilm’s era structure and StarWars.com’s published chronological order for the major screen stories, including the note that Andor and Rebels overlap before Rogue One [page:1][page:2].

Eras

High Republic, Fall of the Jedi, Reign of the Empire, Age of Rebellion, New Republic, and the rise of the First Order form the main narrative spine [page:1].

Characters

Key lifelines show where Anakin/Vader, Luke, Leia, Han, Andor, Boba Fett, Din Djarin, Rey, Finn, Tarkin, and Gideon sit across the saga [page:2].

Stories

The bottom track places the major movies and series in order, from The Acolyte through the sequel trilogy and related shows [page:2].
