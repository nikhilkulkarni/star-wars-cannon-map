Canon years on one axis, release history on the other

This page separates two different questions: when a story happens in canon, and when Lucasfilm released it. The top module lists stories in canon order with the horizontal axis set to BBY/ABY years, and the swap button flips the layout so canon years become columns and stories become movable tiles in a transposed board [page:1][web:61].

Section 1A scrollable story-by-story canon timeline with rows organized by date of happening in canon and labelled with release year [page:1].
Section 2A single button swaps the x and y logic so you can inspect the same data in a transposed view without leaving the page [page:1].
Section 3A 2x2 matrix groups titles by broad canon-era bucket on the x axis and release-era bucket on the y axis, shown as story cards [page:1].

The source list follows StarWars.com’s published chronological viewing order for the major screen canon, including the official notes that Andor and Rebels happen concurrently before Rogue One, and that Resistance begins before The Force Awakens and continues after it [page:1].
